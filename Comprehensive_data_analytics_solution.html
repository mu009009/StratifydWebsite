Comprehensive data analytics solution

The relentless advancement of technology has redefined the relationship between information and analytics. Highly emotional, yet incredibly truncated comments like those you find in Twitter can be critical to brand protection but yield little or no actionable trend information. Application reviews, FaceBook posts and countless other social media based interactions deliver real-time feedback about products, experiences and results. Other communication channels like emails, chat, web-site comment forms, surveys and old fashioned letters are yet another form of customer interaction. The amount of customer facing data generated each day is staggering.

While customer data interaction continues to grow, internal data is even more prevalent. From security logs to sourcing contracts to portfolio planning, enterprise systems create incredible data volumes 24/7/365. Back-end customer systems, B2B transactions and countless other technology integrations yield reams of data about the health and reliability of core systems.

-->

Most organizations are struggling to find analytical solutions that can recognize highly emotional, immediate action trends like those seen in social media applications like Twitter or FaceBook while still providing actionable analytics across greater time intervals. The Signals platform is designed to provide a scalable and extensible data analytics capability across all of your customer and enterprise system data stores.

Your enterprise data has a story, our Signals platform helps you tell it.
